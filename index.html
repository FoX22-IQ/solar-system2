Planets and Sun

Sun
Center of the solar system. A star that gives off heat and light.
Mercury
Smallest planet. Closest to the Sun.
Venus
Hottest planet. Thick atmosphere.
Earth
Only known planet with life. 70% water.
Mars
Red planet. Possible past water.
Jupiter
Largest planet. Has a giant storm.
Saturn
Famous for its rings made of ice.
Uranus
Rotates on its side. Cold gas giant.
Neptune
Strong winds. Deep blue color.
